Step-by-Step Guide to Creating a Firebase Project: Firebase Console and CLI

Prerequisites:
A Google account with billing information linked to it. This is essential for enabling paid Firebase features if needed in the future.

It's worth noting that Firebase's paid plans also include free tiers. In most cases, following this guide will not result in exceeding the free tier.

1:27-:
After creating the firebase project, please enable firestore and storage in firebase console. Otherwise you will be backtracking when you submit the 'firebase init' command in the cli.
